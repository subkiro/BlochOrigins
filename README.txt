BlochOrigins

You challenge your npc opponent. Game finishes as soon as all coins are collected OR someone reache the Finish Blue tile. 
Be careful,  the one who collect more gold and diamonds wins no matter who step on the finish line first.

1. select player and stage
2. when is your turn you select a dice (Normal or Special). special dice is avaliable on 3d turn.
3. whhe you collect Rewinds then you have ability to rewind your movements. so you have a chance to collect more :)
4. if you fall in empty tile, then you are respawn to your initial position.
